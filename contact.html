FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

COMMON QUESTIONS & ANSWERS

How long do the filters last?

Our filters typically last 12 months with regular use. We recommend replacing them annually for optimal performance.

Do I need a plumber for installation?

The under-sink wellness system can be installed in 15 minutes without a plumber. The whole house system requires professional installation.

What contaminants do your systems remove?

Our systems remove a wide range of contaminants including chlorine, lead, fluoride, PFAS, PFOS, heavy metals, bacteria, viruses, and more. View our product pages for detailed information.

What is your warranty policy?

All OMGEN WATER systems come with a comprehensive warranty. Contact us for detailed warranty information specific to your product.
